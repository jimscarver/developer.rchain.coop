REV issuance for RHOC holders
In 2017, the RChain Cooperative created RHOC tokens and sponsored a private sale. At the launch of main-net, February 25, 2020, there was an issuance of REV at a 1:1 ratio based on a holder’s RHOC balance. REV is the utility token of the RChain blockchain. Among other things, REV will allow you to bond a validator’s stake, pay transaction fees, and deploy smart contracts on the new RChain blockchain. If you’re interested in building your own RChain-based dApp, you will need to acquire REV in order to do so.
FAQ+-
What is RHOC?
RHOC is an ERC20 token created on the Ethereum blockchain by the RChain Cooperative on March 20, 2017. The contract address is 0x168296bb09e24a88805cb9c33356536b980d3fc5.

What is REV?
REV is the primary utility token of the RChain blockchain. It allows you to bond a validator’s stake, pay transaction fees, and deploy smart contracts on the RChain blockchain.

When was the REV issuance?
The RChain Main-net launched on February 25, 2020. Upon launch, a genesis block was forged that contained all REV balances.

What does REV issuance mean?
Technically, the event was simply an issuance of all REV tokens, determined by the wallet balances of existing RHOC holders. There was no actual swap. The RHOC will continue to exist on the Ethereum blockchain, even though it is worthless. And you will never need to give your RHOC to anyone to receive your REV. We designed this process to minimize the risk of phishing. So, if anyone asks you to “send your RHOC” to them, you are being phished.

How can I check my REV balance?

How do I acquire REV?
You can acquire REV through OTC trading, exchanges (e.g. MXC, HotBit), or as rewards for being a validator.

How were RHOC balances recorded for the purpose of issuing REV?
The RChain core development team in collaboration with members of the RChain community created a script to collect RHOC balances given a specified ETH block hash. This script is available at https://github.com/rchain/rhoc-balance-reporter. At Ethereum block 9371743, the Co-op published the snapshot collected by the script and made it available for public review.

What does RHOC/REV swap mean?
We no longer use this term in favor of "REV issuance" that more accurately describes the process. The term “token swap” was introduced before a process had been established and it stuck within the community.

My RHOC was stored on an exchange on January 28, 2020 when RHOC balances were finalized.
Exchanges were notified in advance to freeze trading prior to the snapshot. Contact your exchange for instructions on claiming your REV. Kucoin Exchange issued this notice to their customers holding RHOC.

My RHOC was stored in a hardware wallet, is this supported?
As long as you control the private key, this conforms to the criteria mentioned above.
